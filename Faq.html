Frequently Asked Questions

What is We_sell.com? ▼
We_sell.com is an e-commerce platform where you can find a wide range of products including men's wear, women's wear, kids' wear, electronics, food, books, and watches.
How do I place an order? ▼
To place an order, browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to create an account or log in to complete your purchase.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
We accept various payment methods including credit cards, debit cards, PayPal, and other secure online payment options.
How can I track my order? ▼
Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number on our website or the carrier's website to track your package.
What is your return policy? ▼
We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Items must be in their original condition and packaging. Please contact our customer service for return instructions.
How do I contact customer support? ▼
You can contact our customer support team through the chatbot on our website, by email at support@esite.com, or by phone at 1-800-ESITE.
Do you offer international shipping? ▼
Yes, we offer international shipping to select countries. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. Please check our shipping page for more details.
How do I reset my password? ▼
To reset your password, click on "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions to receive a reset link via email.
